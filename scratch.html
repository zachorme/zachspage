Some helpful tips

  1. Arm
    • Hold the bow with your non-dominant hand
    • Bend your arm slightly
    • Don't "death grip" the bow
  2. Fingers
    • Use the tips of your fingers
    • Most professionals use three fingers
    • Two is acceptable
  3. Stance
    • Face your targe 45 degrees on
    • Most begginers are hesitant to face their target
    • Dont be perpendicular to it
